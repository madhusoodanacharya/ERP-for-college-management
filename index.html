About us

Vidya Vikas Institute of Engineering & Technology

The Vidya Vikas Institute of Engineering and Technology is one of the top 10 engineering colleges in Mysore, Karnataka. Affliated to VTU Mysore, our college was founded in 1997 and has now grown rapidly into one of the best engineering colleges in Karnataka. Our courses include BE., MBA, and MCA. Counted as one of the top Engineering colleges in Karnataka for its placement opportunities, we cater to 3000 students and staff members in an environment that is rich in cultural and linguistic diversity.
